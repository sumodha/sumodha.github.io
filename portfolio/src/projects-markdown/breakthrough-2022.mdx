# Introduction 
Breakthrough is a **non-profit** that seeks to **prepare high-achieving middle school students to succeed in demanding high school programs**; support and guide them on their college-bound path; and **encourage the talented college/high school students leading them to explore careers in education**.

## MOTIVATION
As a **Breakthrough student myself** (see more on my Breakthough Journey [here](https://sumodha.github.io/portfolio/breakthrough-2023)), I experienced first hand how pivotal the Breakthrough community was to my success throughout middle and high school. After I completed the student part of the program (until the summer before 10th grade), I knew I **wanted to give back to the community that has given me so much**! So, during the summer before junior year, I **volunteered as a teaching assistant with Breakthrough**. This was a great opportunity for me to **reciprocate the love that Breakthrough gave me** and learn more about what it meant to help out the teaching fellows (the following year I became a teaching fellow... see my experience [here](https://sumodha.github.io/portfolio/breakthrough-2023)).

# Roles & Responsibilities
As a teaching assistant for the Rice Owls Team at Breakthrough, I ...
- assisted and communicated with **30+ teachers and 4+ directors daily**
- wrote **weekly stories** that tested students on their knowledge of new words that they had learned 
- served as a **role model** to students 
- organized activities for **350+ middle school and high school students** 
- helped with **transportation services** 
- attended **daily meetings with directors** to keep up to date with tasks that needed to be done
