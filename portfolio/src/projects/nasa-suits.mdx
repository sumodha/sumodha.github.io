# Overview
[NASA SUITS](https://www.nasa.gov/learning-resources/spacesuit-user-interface-technologies-for-students/) is a design challenge . Our team, OWL SUITS, developed an **augmented reality (AR) interface** on the HoloLens 2 to support astronauts during **extravehicular activities (Ingress/Egress, Navigation, & Geological Sampling)**. The system enhances astronaut performance, safety, and efficiency through interactive visualizations, spatial navigation aids, and real-time data overlays.
